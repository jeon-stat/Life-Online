# Life Online Product Direction

## Product Position

Life Online is not a simple to-do app with a character on top.
It should become a daily growth OS where healthy real-world behavior is translated into:

- visible avatar growth
- a personal life log
- gentle structure for better days
- cosmetic and emotional rewards people care about

## Core Promise

If the user lives a better day in reality, the character and the world should grow in a way that feels fair, motivating, and worth returning to.

## Design Principles

1. Do not treat every activity as equally trustworthy.
2. Use a mixed verification system instead of pretending everything can be auto-proven.
3. Reward balance and consistency instead of grind.
4. Make cosmetic progression and self-reflection as important as raw XP.
5. Keep the app useful even when the user has low energy.

## Verification Tiers

### Tier A: Automatic

Best for strong XP rewards.

- steps
- workout duration
- sleep windows
- focus session duration tied to a timer or app usage signal

### Tier B: Guided / Semi-verified

Good for medium XP rewards.

- planned study session completed
- tidy reset with before/after confirmation
- wake-up check-in followed by a short morning routine
- reading session with timer and recap

### Tier C: Self-log

Small XP, but high emotional value.

- mood check
- gratitude
- short reflection
- social connection log
- intention setting

## Daily Growth Model

### Daily XP Limits

- total daily XP cap: 100
- body cap: 30
- focus cap: 30
- mind cap: 20
- life cap: 20

### Diminishing Returns

Repeated actions should give less XP:

- first completion: 100%
- second completion: 60%
- third or later: 25%

### Reward Philosophy

- automatic data should feel valuable
- self-logs should support reflection, not replace real behavior
- diversity bonus is better than repetition bonus

## Character Strategy

Start with one high-quality base avatar.

Do not launch with many classes as the primary structure.
Use:

- one default avatar
- level-based evolution
- unlockable cosmetics
- later personality branches based on behavior patterns

### Evolution Layers

- early levels: cleaner silhouette, posture, expression
- mid levels: clothing and accessory unlocks
- later levels: background, room, companion, aura

## App Structure

### Today

The daily entry point.

- daily growth summary
- avatar state
- recommended actions
- verified actions
- one-sentence status log

### Focus

The action screen for meaningful sessions.

- focus session launcher
- recommended study / reading blocks
- distraction notes
- progress toward daily focus target

### Journey

The life log and reflection archive.

- category totals
- recent completed actions
- weekly highlights
- mood and reflection history

### Avatar

The motivational reward layer.

- current avatar stage
- next evolution preview
- unlocked and upcoming cosmetics
- future room / pet / style hooks

## Monetization Direction

Avoid selling raw power directly.

Good monetization:

- seasonal outfits
- room themes
- companions / pets
- emotes and effects
- premium journals and visual summaries
- themed challenge passes

Avoid:

- direct XP sales
- pay-to-level boosts
- premium verification shortcuts

## MVP Recommendation

Launch with:

- Today
- Focus
- Journey
- Avatar

First meaningful actions:

- walk goal
- focus session
- wind-down / sleep prep
- tidy reset
- daily reflection

## Prototype Notes

The current prototype should shift away from "random buttons that grant growth" and toward:

- category-based daily limits
- more realistic action labels
- menu-driven navigation
- visible avatar progression hooks
- a retained history of actions
